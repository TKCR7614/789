Purpose and benefits

This page will explain the reasons for collecting data on student progress at GIC.

purpose

The purpose of collecting the data on student progress at GIC is to know how students feel when study in GIC and what progress have been made. It helps us to improve the services in GIC

benefits

The benefits of collecting the data on student progress at GIC is that it can help us improve the style and quality of the class and other arrangements. Through this we can improve the overall student experience.
Collecting data on student progress at GIC can also reflect each student's growth process.
